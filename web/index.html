About This Project

Finds diamonds in the dirt. A fundamentals-driven engine focused on small-mid cap equities ($300M–$20B) — the under-researched, undervalued, quality companies that institutional coverage overlooks.

Every stock is scored across five dimensions:

  • Strong fundamentals (Piotroski F-Score)
  • DCF valuation with margin of safety
  • Low analyst coverage (institutional blindspot)
  • Positive free cash flow trends
  • ROIC exceeding cost of capital

Avoid the mega-caps everyone owns. Find the next great company before Wall Street does.

About This Model

SignalBoard scores every stock across eight quantitative dimensions, each normalized to 0–100:

  • Income Health — revenue trajectory, earnings persistence, and peer-relative growth
  • Cash Flow — free cash flow yield and trend consistency
  • Piotroski F-Score — financial strength (profitability, leverage, efficiency)
  • ROIC Spread — return on invested capital minus weighted average cost of capital
  • DCF Valuation — intrinsic value vs market price with margin of safety
  • Balance Sheet — debt-to-equity, current ratio, net cash position
  • Margins — gross and operating margin quality vs sector peers
  • Blindspot — low analyst coverage signals institutional under-ownership

A composite score weights these dimensions and ranks the universe daily. The ensemble ML model (LightGBM) then assigns BUY/SELL probabilities using walk-forward validation with isotonic calibration.

Target horizon: 10 trading days. Retrained monthly. Vol-scaled thresholds per ticker.

How It Works

A quantitative pipeline that screens ~3,000 US equities through safety filters, scores them across seven fundamental dimensions, and ranks the survivors using a regime-aware composite model.

Pipeline

Universe ~3,000 US equities across all sectors and market caps
Safety Filters Altman Z > 3.0 · Market cap $300M–$20B · 2+ quarters of data
Feature Engineering 100+ signals across 12 categories: technical, fundamental, macro, DCF, microstructure, cross-sectional, calendar, network, statistical, options, interaction, deep fundamentals
ML Model LightGBM multiclass classifier (UP / FLAT / DOWN) trained via walk-forward temporal validation with 2-year windows and purge gaps. Isotonic probability calibration.
Composite Scoring 7-dimension weighted score normalized 0–100, dynamically adjusted by macro regime (VIX-based risk-on / risk-off)
Ranking Top 15 stocks ranked by calibrated composite score. Retrained monthly with vol-scaled thresholds per ticker.

Regime Awareness

VIX-based regime detection adjusts dimension weights dynamically. Defensive posture in volatile markets, opportunistic in calm ones.

Risk-On VIX < 15
↑ Growth Momentum weight ↑ DCF Upside weight ↓ Balance Sheet weight
Risk-Off VIX > 25
↑ Balance Sheet weight ↑ Cash Flow weight ↓ Growth Momentum weight
