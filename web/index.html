About SignalBoard

Finds diamonds in the dirt. A fundamentals-driven engine focused on small-mid cap equities ($300M–$20B) — the under-researched, undervalued, quality companies that institutional coverage overlooks.

Every stock is scored across five dimensions:

  • Strong fundamentals (Piotroski F-Score)
  • DCF valuation with margin of safety
  • Low analyst coverage (institutional blindspot)
  • Positive free cash flow trends
  • ROIC exceeding cost of capital

Avoid the mega-caps everyone owns. Find the next great company before Wall Street does.
